What are we doing?
Re-creating Runescape in the early 5th age through Neverwinter Nights 2, give our collective take to a game we love dearly. Molior RS is a living, breathing world, with a thousand possibilities, expansive lore-based quests, player vs player dueling, bossing, and many skills to train. An exciting new experience awaits you!

"Simply put, Molior RS aims to be one of the first living pieces of MMORPG fanfiction." - PC GAMER

The Bulk of it
Through countless hours of area design, script writing, quest building, and programming, we've developed a vast amount of gameplay within lore-based quests, combat, skills, classic locations, and many new lands to explore. We aren't duplicating Runescape, we're reconstructing it based on the lore, so you'll be playing the game you remember but at the same time you won't. With the Aurora Engine's toolset in our hands, we can make the project endless. Molior Rs is free to play, but you need to own Neverwinter Nights 2: complete Edition because Molior RS is a mod for Neverwinter Nights 2.

We have been Jagex-approved, and are a Proud Runescape community project!

Some Important things to note

  • Molior RS is NOT a Runescape Private Server.

  • Molior RS is NOT intended to replace the Runescape experience, it's supplemental.

  • Molior RS does NOT use any of Runescape's source code.
